# Contributing to traigent-schema

Thanks for your interest in contributing.

## Contributor License Agreement (required)

`traigent-schema` is **dual-licensed** (AGPL-3.0-only **or** a Traigent commercial license).
For Traigent Ltd to continue offering the project under **both** licenses, it must hold
sufficient rights in every contribution. Therefore **all contributions require a signed
[Contributor License Agreement](CLA.md)** granting Traigent broad inbound rights, including the
right to sublicense/relicense your contribution under both the AGPL and commercial terms.

- A Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO) sign-off alone is **not** sufficient here, because it
  does not grant the relicensing rights dual-licensing requires.
- Until the CLA process is live and you have signed, **external pull requests cannot be merged.**
  (Internal Traigent employee contributions are covered by employment IP assignment.)

> CLA tooling (e.g. a CLA-assistant check on PRs) will be wired into CI as a follow-up; this file
> documents the policy.

## Development

See [`README.md`](README.md) for setup, validation, and test instructions. Keep changes scoped,
add/adjust tests, and run the validators before opening a PR.

## What not to commit

This repo is published as the **open-source (AGPL)** face of the schema. Do **not** add Traigent
proprietary material, internal-only docs, or the commercial-agreement text here — the binding
commercial terms live in the customer agreement maintained by Traigent legal, not in this repo.
